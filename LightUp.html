LightUp

Never Forget to Turn Off Lights Now!



 Type: Individual Academic Project
Duration: Sept - Nov 2015
Tools: Paper prototype, Illustrator, Marvel

Overview

Problem Statement

Studies have shown that each year the US wastes over 61% of its energy produced, 26% of which is due to electricity wastage. To counter this wastage, 15 million trees will have to be planted, which will acquire an area twice as big as Ann Arbor. There is a sheer requirement of energy saving practices to be adopted in people’s daily lives. Students and professors at University of Michigan admit forgetting to switch of lights or fans frequently while rushing for the classes and end up paying high electricity bills. At a broader level, this practice is continuously undermining the efforts of global energy savings.

Solution Overview

Out of several solutions to the problem, the one that worked out to be most relevant was providing a mobile app to notify users of appliances left turned on when they leave. Following the notification, user can switch off the appliance and monitor the daily savings. The application would also provide tips on energy savings and comparative performance statistics in the neighbourhood. Also, sharing such information would enable spreading general awareness among youths to imbibe better efficiency practices in their daily lives.
