Class Aware

A device to increase awareness about the classroom activity



 Type: Tangible Interaction Design
Time: October 2015 (Ongoing)
~ Brainstorming, Research, Ideation, Prototyping ~

Overview

Project Goal

To increase awareness of teaching and learning activities within a department among instructors to build a sense of community and increase discussion of pedagogy. More than half of instructors at University of Michigan agreed to the fact that they had no idea what the students are doing in the class or if they are interested at all.


Solution Overview

A tangible device or a digital product that informs instructors about the studentâ€™s activities in class or off-class through visual or audio feedback. Determining in-class attentiveness through gaze-tracking and observing course involvment via university portal would keep instructor aware of the overall class engagement.


Role

The team comprises of 3 students and a mentor. My role is to deifne and incorporate product features and design user interface to create better user experience and achieve the desired goal. Studied over a dozen related works to frame relevant product features. Also responsible for bringing in space design perspective to better understand awareness in the physical learning environment.



Design & Research



Related Work

I conducted research on more than a dozen related works to analyse methods and modalities developed recently to increase ambient awareness. The broad classification of the findings were as follows:
What is the type of User Experience?
Is the visual/audio signal abstract or direct?
Is the signal helping in decision making?
What is the user's level of interaction?
Is there a direct or indirect mental mapping?
