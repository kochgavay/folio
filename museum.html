Amazon Discover

A feature app from Amazon to enhance social experience



 Type: Group Project
Time: October 2015
Brainstorming, Ideation, Prototyping

Project Goal

While waiting in a queue or at a station, people usually open up social media apps like Twitter or Instagram. Amazon wanted to include a feature with Social Experience to engage users in such situations even when they do not intend to shop.


Solution

Proposed gamification of the shopping experience with single or multiplayer games that can let customers win discount coupons. Also, proposed a news feed scroll that informs users about latest products and their most attractive feature sorted on popularity.


Role

In a team of 6, I collaborated with a teammate to work on gamification ideas and sketching the interface design. I also worked on the card designs for the Discover feature.


Brainstorming

Brainstorming ideas for engaging users while they are not on task and waiting in a queue or standing at a station. We created different scenarios where users might be interested to open up the app even when they do not intend to shop. For Amazon to be a priority, it was imperative to provide an incentive for shopping.



Ideation

We came up with various solutions including gamification of the shopping experience and providing stumble discovery scroll. Scanning any apparel near you, using augmented reality, would make it faster and easier for users to buy something they really liked.



Amazon Games

Small single or multiplayer games could keep users busy and distracted from their boredom and also offer discount coupons in exchange.





Amazon Discover

Important features of the page:
An interactive feed of Amazon products.
Attractive single page image to grab user’s attention.
Fun facts so that they stay longer on the page.
Price and availability of the item listed next to it, should the user want to buy it.
A user “Thumbs up” option to express feedback.



Takeaways

Rapid Prototyping

Producing quick prototypes in a short timeline to convey the intent

Empathy

Evaluating users problem and cater to their needs

Teamwork

Working strategically with team members to efficiently produce desired results
